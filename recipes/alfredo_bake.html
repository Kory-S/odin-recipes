Cheesy Chicken Alfredo Pasta Bake

Easy cheesy chicken alfredo pasta bake recipe - loaded with cheese, pasta, tender chicken.

Ingredients:

    Makes 6 servings

    Prep Time: 15 min

    Cook Time: 35 min

    Total Time: 50 min

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 chikcen breasts cubed
  • salt/pepper to taste
  • 4 cloves garlic minced
  • 2 ½ cups chicken broth
  • ½lb penne pasta uncooked
  • 2 cups parmesan cheese (separated 1½ cups and ½ cups)
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese shredded> (seperated into 1 cup and 1 cup)
  • optional 1 handful fresh parsley chopped

Instructions

  1. Prep pot
    2. heat oil and add Chicken.
  2. Season chicken
    3. Salt and pepper to taste, then add garlic.
  3. Brown chicken
    4. Cook until chicken is brown and cooked through.
  4. Add additional ingredients
    5. Add broth, heavy cream, and pasta. Bring to a boil.
  5. Boil
    6. Bring to a boil
  6. Cover and simmer
    7. Cover and reduce the heat to a medium. Simmer for 15-20 min.
  7. Add parmaesan
    8. Add 1½ cups parmesan cheese.
  8. Add pasta to pan
    9. Pour half of the pasta into a greased 11x7 or 9x13 baking dish.
  9. Add mozzarella
    10. sprinkle evenly with 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  10. Add remaining pasta to pan
    11. Pour the remaining half of the pasta evenly on top. Sprinkle evenly with other 1 cup of mozzarella and ½ parmesan cheese.
  11. Broil>
    12. Broil 10-15 min, or until cheese is golden brown. Remove and sprinkle with fresh parsley.
