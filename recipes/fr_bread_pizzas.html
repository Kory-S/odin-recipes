Oven-Baked French Bread Pizzas

Build-your-own-pizza. French bread pizzas are easy and everyone can make their own personal favorite with little work!

Ingredients:

    Makes 4 servings

    Prep Time: 15 min

    Cook Time: 10 min

    Total Time: 25 min

    Margherita Pizza Toppings

  • 1 baguette sliced in half crosswise and lengthwise making 4 total pieces
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • ¾ cup shredded mozzarella
  • Optional 1 tbsp fresh basil chopped
  • Optional½ tsp red pepper flakes

    • BBQ Chicken Pizza Toppings

  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1 cup shredded rotisserie chicken
  • ¾ cup shredded mozzarella
  • Optional 2 tbsp sliced red onions

Instructions:

  1. Prep
    2. Preheat oven to 350°F(180°C) and line 2 baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. Setup
    3. Arrange baguette pieces on baking sheets, cut-side up
  3. Margherita Pizzas
    4. Spread 2 baguette pices with tomato sauce. Top with shredded mozzarella, cherry tomatoes, mozzarella balls, and basil.
  4. BBQ Chicken Pizzas
    5. Spread 2 baguette pieces with BBQ sauce. Top with shredded chicken, shredded mozzarella, and red onion.
  5. Bake>
    6. Bake until cheese is browned and bubbling - about 10 min.
  6. Garnish
    7. Margherita Pizzas: chopped basil | BBQ Chicken Pizzas: red pepper flakes.
